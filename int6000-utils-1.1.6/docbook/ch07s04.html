Busybox™

Busybox™ is a monolithic application program that implements most standard GNU™ utilities included in many Linux™ distributions. The AMiLDA™ distribution used by Intellon includes Busybox-1.10.0 and Linux-2.4.32. These two components provide a nearly complete operating environment.

Intellon has made no changes to Linux™ and only a few changes Busybox™ as provided.
